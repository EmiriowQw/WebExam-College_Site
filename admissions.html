Admissions

Start your journey to becoming an engineer at Engineering College. We welcome passionate and dedicated students from diverse backgrounds who are ready to innovate and make a difference.

Why Apply to Engineering College?

Engineering College offers a transformative educational experience that prepares students for successful careers in engineering. Our admission process is designed to identify students who demonstrate academic excellence, creativity, and a strong commitment to engineering.

We consider each application holistically, looking beyond test scores to understand your unique strengths, experiences, and potential contributions to our community.

Rolling Admissions

Applications reviewed year-round with multiple intake periods.

Merit-Based Scholarships

Financial support available for qualifying students.

Fast Processing

Decisions typically within 2-3 weeks of completed application.

Support Services

Dedicated admissions counselors to guide you through the process.

Application Process

Follow these steps to complete your application to Engineering College.

Complete Online Application

Fill out our comprehensive online application form with your personal information, academic background, and program preferences. The application takes approximately 20-30 minutes to complete.

Submit Required Documents

Upload the following documents:

  • High school transcripts or equivalent certificates
  • Standardized test scores (SAT, ACT, or equivalent)
  • Two letters of recommendation (preferably from math or science teachers)
  • Personal statement or essay (500-750 words)
  • Copy of valid identification

Pay Application Fee

Submit the non-refundable application fee of $75. Fee waivers are available for students with financial need. Contact our admissions office for more information.

Interview (If Required)

Selected applicants may be invited for an interview with our admissions committee. Interviews can be conducted in person or virtually and typically last 30-45 minutes.

Receive Decision

You will receive an admission decision via email within 2-3 weeks of submitting your complete application. Accepted students will receive detailed information about enrollment and next steps.

Confirm Enrollment

Accepted students must confirm their enrollment by submitting the enrollment deposit and completing the registration process by the specified deadline.

Admission Requirements

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale
  • Strong background in mathematics and science
  • Completion of prerequisite courses:
    • 4 years of Mathematics (including Calculus)
    • 3 years of Science (including Physics and Chemistry)
    • 4 years of English

Standardized Tests

Required (choose one):

  • SAT: Minimum score of 1200 (Math + Reading)
  • ACT: Minimum composite score of 25
  • International equivalent exams

English Proficiency (for international students):

  • TOEFL: Minimum score of 80 (iBT)
  • IELTS: Minimum score of 6.5
