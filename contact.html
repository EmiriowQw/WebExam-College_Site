Frequently Asked Questions

Admission requirements include a high school diploma, minimum GPA of 3.0, standardized test scores (SAT/ACT), letters of recommendation, and a personal statement. Visit our Admissions page for detailed requirements.

We have rolling admissions with priority deadlines. For Fall intake: Priority deadline is March 1, Regular deadline is June 15. For Spring intake: Priority deadline is October 1, Regular deadline is December 1.
