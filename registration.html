What Happens Next?

Confirmation Email

You'll receive an immediate confirmation email with your application ID and login credentials for the applicant portal.

Document Submission

Upload required documents (transcripts, test scores, recommendations) through your applicant portal within 7 days.

Application Review

Our admissions committee will review your complete application. This process typically takes 2-3 weeks.

Admission Decision

You'll receive an email notification with your admission decision and next steps for accepted students.

Enrollment

Accepted students can confirm their enrollment by paying the deposit and completing registration for classes.
